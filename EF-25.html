EF-25

Multi Stage Indirect Heat & Dry Decomposition Process

EF-25 is made for use in medical-scale restaurants & cafes, Mid-sized apartments, fast-food franchises, canteens and food preparation facilities in institutions and cafeterias.

It has a capacity to process 25 to 30 kg input of food waste per day. The process takes 18-24 hours for completion. The equipment is very compact in size and built-in stainless steel for corrosion resistance and durability.

01

In-built shredder

It is the ﬁrst process that the wet waste passes into the ECOFRIEND. Shredding process helps reduction of particle size which can make the process easy and get the required volume reduction in the minimal time, it saves electricity and makes ﬁnal product in the powder form.

02

Heating Pad based heating system

Fibber glass jacket heater type heater having long life which reduces main part of maintenance of fully automatic composting machine. It' 64 density ceramic wool gives us shock proof feature and it’s become main safety features. Zero percent heat loss will decrease on time of heater and increase power saving.

03

Master & Slave heating system

Prevents downtime if a heater failure occurs. The slave heaters come on in case of the master heater failure. There is no need to shut down the machine when a heater failure occurs.

04

Direct drive technology

The machines use a direct drive technology wherein the main shaft of the machine on which mixing blades are fixed is directly coupled to the geared motor by a perfectly matched planetary gearbox. The shaft with the mixing blades is balanced with precision, resulting in better load distribution to the main shaft and better mixing. It also prevents main shaft breakage due to metal fatigue and stress.

05

Motors and controls

All motors and control equipment are of international quality, resulting in better efficiency and durability.
