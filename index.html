Process

  • Segregation

    Waste segregation means dividing the waste into Dry (Recyclable) and Wet. Dry waste includes Glass, Metal, Wood, Electronics, Plastic products .

  • Feeding

    After segregation the dry (recyclable) and wet waste, the wet waste is fed into the machine through openable door at the top.

  • Inbuilt Shredding

    It is the ﬁrst process that the wet waste passes into the ECOFRIEND.

  • Conversion

    After the Wet waste is shredded, it enters in the tank where the actual conversion takes place through our unique mixing, air circulation and heating mechanism.

  • Soil Supplement Removal

    Can be done every 24 hours through a wide removal outlet
